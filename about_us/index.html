Overview

Millasky General Trading FZC - a general trading company, based in Ajman, United Arab Emirates since 2003. . We deal in the wholesale distribution of Household products, Cleaning Supplies, and Personal Care products. We distribute all our products to more than 1000 retail outlets across the UAE – namely Hypermarkets, Coops, Supermarkets, Petrol Stations, Gyms, Schools and Groceries.
